How do we get our data?

The website uses a custom compiled dataset featuring the 250 most well known movies. The information is structured to provide users with an easy and clear overview of each film.

To complement the content, an external API is also used to access information from the Bechdel Test. This allows for comparisons between traditional ratings and gender-based representation in movies.

This combination offers a balanced and informative presentation of the films based on both popularity and content analysis.
